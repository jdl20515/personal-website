SupaSole

As active runners, our team had the problem of having to buy new shoes every few months due to wear and tear due to running. This resulted in us having to spend a fortune on shoes, and it became a large problem in our life, motiviating us to create SupaSole. Founded just a few months ago, SupaSole is a solution to this widespread problem. Through market research, we found that the outsole of the shoe is the part that breaks down the fastest, so we developed a replacable outsole with good durability and traction. Though we are still trying to start production, you can find more information by emailing me or going to our website.
